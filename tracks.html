Smart Tracks

Precision-engineered tracks for various construction and industrial applications

Refined Metal Smart Tracks

Refined Metal Smart Tracks are U-shaped channels that provide the foundation for steel framing systems. Acting as the top and bottom runners, they hold studs securely in place, keeping walls straight, stable, and aligned. Whether you're building heavy-duty structural walls, light interior partitions, or value-engineered systems, Refined Metal offers a full range of track solutions to match your project needs.

Structural Tracks

Structural Tracks are designed for strength and stability in load-bearing wall systems. They are paired with our Structural Smart Studs to create frames capable of supporting significant vertical and lateral loads. With a wide range of depths and flange sizes, our Structural Tracks provide flexibility for everything from mid-rise construction to heavy-duty wall assemblies.

Non-Structural Tracks

Non-Structural Tracks are built for interior, non-load-bearing framing such as drywall partitions, soffits, and furring systems. They provide a lightweight yet durable base for our Non-Structural Smart Studs, making them easy to handle on-site while keeping walls aligned and consistent.

Flange (Leg) Sizes

18 (25), 27 (22), 30 (20)

Standard Length

8ft, 10ft. Custom Length Available

EQ Tracks

EQ Tracks are designed as a smarter alternative to traditional 20-gauge tracks, delivering the same performance at a lighter weight. Manufactured from higher-yield steel, they reduce material use without compromising strength. EQ Tracks pair with our EQ Studs to create cost-effective framing systems perfect for interior drywall applications.

SPECIFICATION

Web Depths

1 5/8", 2 1/2", 3 5/8", 6"

Flange (Leg) Sizes

1 1/4"

Thickness Mil (Gauge)

19 (EQ 20Ga), 15 (EQ 25Ga), 13 (EQ 25Ga)

Standard Length

8ft, 10ft. Custom Length Available
