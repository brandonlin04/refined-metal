Refined Metal Smart Decking

Refined Metal Smart Decking systems are precision-engineered cold-formed steel panels designed for floor and roof deck applications. Manufactured under strict ICC-ES quality control programs, our decking complies with the 2024 and 2021 IBC/IRC requirements, providing reliable structural support and excellent performance in residential, commercial, and industrial construction.

Our decking systems feature optimized profiles and configurations to provide superior structural performance, excellent spanning capabilities, and ease of installation. Perfect for floor systems, roof decking, and structural applications requiring long-span performance and superior load-carrying capacity.
