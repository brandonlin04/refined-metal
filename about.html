About Refined Metal

Your trusted partner in steel solutions for construction

Our Story

Refined Metal was founded with one goal: to provide dependable, competitively priced steel solutions for the construction industry. Based in New York, we are proud to manufacture close to our customers, reducing delays and ensuring your projects stay on schedule.

Our product range spans from roll-formed studs, tracks, joists, and decking to doors, railings, and fully custom fabrications. Whether you need standard products or tailored solutions, our flexibility ensures you get exactly what your project demands without compromise.

Unlike traditional distributors and wholesalers, Refined Metal works directly with contractors and builders, cutting down lead times and lowering costs. With a commitment to quality, efficiency, and service, we deliver the strength and reliability you can trust to build with confidence.
