Structural support for floors and roofs in modern construction

Refined Metal Smart Joists

Refined Metal Smart Joists are cold-formed steel members designed to span long distances while carrying heavy loads. Acting as the backbone for floor and roof systems, our joists deliver the strength of structural steel in a lightweight, easy-to-install profile.

Compared to traditional wood joists, steel joists offer greater consistency, higher strength-to-weight ratios, and long-term resistance to warping, shrinking, or termite damage. They are the go-to choice for projects where durability, precision, and design flexibility are required.

Structural Joists

Refined Metal Structural Joists are engineered for load-bearing applications, pairing perfectly with our Structural Smart Studs and Tracks to complete a high-strength framing system. Manufactured in multiple depths and thicknesses, these joists provide solutions for a wide range of structural demands, from small commercial floors to large roof spans.
